F.A.Q
Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download the mobile banking app?

To download our app, simply visit your device's app store (App Store for iOS or Google Play for Android), search for our banking app, and click 'Install.' Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

Is my financial information safe when using this app?

Yes, we prioritize your security. Our app uses advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your financial data. Rest assured, your information is kept confidential and secure.

What should I do if I forget my app login credentials?

If you forget your login credentials, you can easily reset them through the app. Just click on the 'Forgot Password' or 'Forgot Username' option on the login screen, and follow the prompts to regain access to your account.

Are there any fees associated with using the app?

We offer a range of pricing plans, including a free option with basic features. However, some advanced features may be available through premium plans with associated fees. Please refer to our pricing section for more details.

Can I use the app for international transactions?

Yes, our app allows for international transactions, including currency conversion and wire transfers. However, please check the associated fees and exchange rates, which may apply to international transactions.
